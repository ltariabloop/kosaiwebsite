Strategy Meets Operations

KOSAI is a consulting studio for sports, wellness, and hospitality. Strategy is paired with the discipline of daily operation: clear positioning, precise planning, and teams that deliver. Projects open well, run smoothly, and compound value. Places that feel effortless to guests and remain exact to manage.

End-to-End Excellence

Leaders in Fitness & Wellness

With extensive experience managing over 100+ gyms, 35+ football fields and stadiums, 700+ basketball courts and arenas, and 800+ badminton facilities, KOSAI Wellness and Consulting has established itself as a leader in fitness and wellness facility management. Our portfolio spans diverse sports and recreational facilities including tennis courts, track facilities, futsal courts, volleyball courts, and specialized venues for pickleball, padel, and bowling clubs.

As we forge ahead into the future, our key focus remains steadfast. We drive wellbeing through all our offerings, from comprehensive sports facility management to retail and restaurant operations, while embedding sustainability and operational excellence across every facility we manage.

Discover the world with our collection of brands that offer a holistic approach to your wellbeing. Every experience we design expands perspectives and awakens transformation to advance the way we live.
