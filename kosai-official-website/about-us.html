About Us

Strategy Meets Operations

KOSAI is a consulting studio for sports, wellness, and hospitality. Strategy is paired with the discipline of daily operation: clear positioning, precise planning, and teams that deliver. Projects open well, run smoothly, and compound value. Places that feel effortless to guests and remain exact to manage.

View our Hotels & Resorts

We work end-to-end—feasibility and finance, concept and design, recruitment and training, launch and growth. The result is alignment: product, people, and process moving as one. Measurable uplift without the noise: higher usage, healthier margins, stronger advocacy.

End-to-End Excellence

Leaders in Fitness & Wellness

Accumulating over 3,000 industry awards, KOSAI Wellness and Consulting proudly sits among the Top 25 Hotel Brands in the World according to Travel + Leisure. Our hotels and resorts have received numerous awards in recognition of the team’s commitment to delivering service excellence and introducing travellers to the most awe-inspiring corners of the world.

As we forge ahead into the future, our key focus remains steadfast. We drive wellbeing through all our offerings, from our Wellbeing Sanctuaries to our brand new app Beyond, while embedding sustainability through our Stay For Good programme.

Discover the world with our collection of brands that offer a holistic approach to your wellbeing. Every experience we design expands perspectives and awakens transformation to advance the way we live.
