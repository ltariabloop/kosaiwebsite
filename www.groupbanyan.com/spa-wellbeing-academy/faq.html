    Where is the Academy located?

    Banyan Spa & Wellbeing Academy is situated on the grounds of KOSAI Phuket within the Laguna Phuket resort complex.

    Can I apply for a course without any prior experience?

    The course is adapted for all levels of experience whether beginner, intermediate or advanced. 

    What is included in the course?

    The course includes learning materials and raw ingredients used during practice sessions. Breaktime refreshments including tea, coffee & cookies are provided. Upon completion of the course, a Diploma Certificate will be provided by Banyan Spa & Wellbeing Academy.


    The course does not include transportation or accommodation. However, we can advise on both matters. For those driving a vehicle, there is complimentary on-site parking. Meals can be provided at additional cost with 2 meals per day (cost depends on the course duration).

    How do I enroll?

    Reach out directly to Banyan Spa & Wellbeing Academy

    E-mail: saowanee.k@groupbanyan.com
    Line ID: 063-385-8667

    Office Phone: 076-372-400 Extension #6612

    Please prepare the following documents for the application:
     1 Copy of National ID Card
     1 Copy of House Registration
     2 Photos - 1 x 1 inch
     1 Copy of Education Qualifications

    How long in advance should I register for the couse?

    Please register at least one week in advance. 

    What language is the course taught in?

    All courses are taught in the Thai language. However, the Thai Foundation (60 hours) and Foundation Aroma Massage (90 hours) can be taught in English. 

    When will I receive the certification?

    Within 1 week of completing the course. 

    What can I do with the diploma?

    Upon completion, submit the Diploma Certificate to the Health Ministry to be officially registered as a licensed therapist (Applicable for Thai Nationals only with 150 hours of Thai Massage training).

    Job opportunities after graduation with KOSAI?

    The Diploma Certificate of Thai Massage (150 hours) is certified by the Ministry of Health, and with additional experience in another course (for example Aromatherapy 90 hours), it is possible to apply for relevant job vacancies.

    What should I wear?

    Please wear a white top or polo shirt, dark-coloured loose trousers, and trainers (white, black or grey).
